HOW TO PLAY

Sit in a circle around a center cup. On your turn, draw a card and do what it says, then pass to the next player. Each card has a rule:

A — Waterfall: everyone drinks; you can't stop until the person to your right stops.
2 — You: point at someone, they drink.
3 — Me: you drew it, you drink.
4 — Floor: last person to touch the floor drinks.
5 — Guys: all the guys drink.
6 — Chicks: all the girls drink.
7 — Heaven: last person to raise a hand drinks.
8 — Mate: pick a drinking buddy who drinks whenever you do.
9 — Rhyme: say a word; go around rhyming. First to fail drinks.
10 — Categories: pick a category; name things in turn. First to blank drinks.
J — Never Have I Ever: three fingers; anyone who's done it drops one and drinks.
Q — Question Master: answer the drawer's questions and you drink — until the next queen.
K — King's Cup: pour into the center cup. The fourth king drinks it all — game over.
