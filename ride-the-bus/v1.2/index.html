HOW TO PLAY

Get through four guesses in a row to make it off the bus. Miss one and you drink, then start the bus over from the first card.

1 · Red or Black

Guess the colour of the first card.

2 · Higher or Lower

Will the next card be higher or lower than your first? A tie is safe.

3 · Inside or Outside

Will the third card fall between your first two cards, or outside them? Landing on an edge is safe.

4 · Pick the Suit

Name the suit of the fourth card.

Drinking

Miss on a stage and you drink that many sips (stage 1 = 1 … stage 4 = 4), then the bus restarts. Clear all four and you're off the bus — hand out 4 sips to whoever you like.
