GUZZLING GAMES

A growing collection of party games — one site, no setup.

What is this?

Guzzling Games is a single home for a family of party games. Liquid Logic — the Jeopardy-style trivia showdown — leads the lineup, joined by classics like Kings Cup and Ride the Bus and the BFL beer-football stat sheet. Every game plays right in your browser with nothing to install. Where it makes sense, games share the same phone-as-buzzer system so everyone plays from their own device.
